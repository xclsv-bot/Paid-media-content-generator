# UX Audit — Jul 14, 2026

A code-verified audit of the app's workflow UX, prioritized. Every finding cites
the files that exhibit it (line numbers as of this writing — re-grep before
acting). Findings 1–2 turn on product decisions and **need an owner** before
implementation; 3–6 are ready to execute cold.

Context: the audit was commissioned alongside the winner-breakdown feature
(migration `0030`, `src/lib/loop/breakdowns*.ts`), which closes the biggest gap
found — winners were reaching Ideate only as truncated raw text, and concepts
manually marked "Winner" fed nothing. That work also shipped the loop-closing
quick wins (slot chips and "Ideate from this winner" links deep-link into
Ideate carrying org + seed). What remains is below.

## 1. Three "approved / delivered" vocabularies that don't talk *(needs owner — biggest workflow hole)*

There are three independent notions of a finished cut, and no ordering between
them:

- **`approvals.state`** (`Pending | Approved | Changes requested`) — written by
  staff `/review` and by the client's ContentCard
  (`src/app/api/creatives/[id]/approval/route.ts:5`).
- **`deliverables.production_status`** — includes both `Approved` **and**
  `Delivered` (`src/lib/deliverables.ts:3-10`).
- **`/review`'s own definition of "delivered"** — simply "has ≥1 video"
  (`src/app/(staff)/review/page.tsx:72-74`), ignoring `production_status`.

Consequences, all verified:
- **Approving in Review does not publish to the client.** The client library
  keys on `production_status = 'Delivered'` (`src/lib/client/data.ts:112,
  118-121`); Review approval only writes `approvals.state`
  (`src/components/ReviewBoard.tsx:42-65`). A staffer who clears the whole
  Review queue has published nothing until each deliverable is separately
  flipped to `Delivered` on This Week or Queue. Review's own copy ("it stays…
  in the client library") overpromises.
- **`production_status = 'Approved'` is inert.** Nothing gates on it — not
  Review, not the client portal. It is selectable everywhere
  (`src/components/DeliverableStatusSelect.tsx`, `WeekBoard.tsx:316-318`) and
  does nothing.
- **`Delivered` can bypass review entirely.** Nothing requires an approval
  before the flip that exposes a cut to the client.

**Recommendation:** pick one spine. Either Review approval advances
`production_status` (Approved → auto-Delivered, or Delivered gated on an
existing approval), or drop `Approved` from `production_status` and treat
`approvals` as the single review record. Requires a product call on whether
client publication should ever precede internal approval.

## 2. Client approval collides with staff review *(needs owner)*

Staff Review and the client ContentCard share the same `approvals` row and the
same `ReviewCard` component (`src/components/client/ContentCard.tsx:81`):

- A **client approving their own content silently removes it from the staff
  `/review` queue** (Review filters `state !== "Approved"`,
  `src/app/(staff)/review/page.tsx:77`) — staff never see it again.
- Client **"Request changes" routes nowhere**: it sets `approvals.state`, which
  no downstream surface (creator queue, This Week) consumes for re-work.
- Comment authorship is binary "You" vs "XCLSV" (`ReviewCard.tsx:101`) — staff
  can't distinguish client comments from other staff.

**Recommendation:** separate client approval from staff review (two rows or a
`decided_by_role` column), route "Changes requested" into the assignee's queue,
and label comment authors by name/org.

## 3. Missing failure/success feedback *(ready to execute)*

- **`ScriptPanel.save` throws into the void** — no catch, no error surface
  (`src/components/ScriptPanel.tsx:108-122`); **`approveLatest` ignores the
  response entirely** (`:124-133`). A failed save or approve looks identical to
  success. Add the busy/error pattern already used by `VerdictSelect.tsx`.
- **WeekBoard inline edits and `DeliverableStatusSelect`** refresh silently on
  success and only show a shared error line on failure
  (`src/components/WeekBoard.tsx:137-145`). A brief "saved ✓" flash (the
  IdeateWorkspace `flash()` pattern) closes the loop.
- **Transcription is client-side best-effort** — fired after upload from the
  browser (`src/components/VideoUploader.tsx:67`); closing the tab loses the
  transcript, and transcripts now feed winner breakdowns. Move the trigger
  server-side (e.g. `after()` on the video-register route, the pattern the
  breakdown refresher uses) or add a visible "transcript pending — retry"
  affordance.

## 4. Status Babel *(ready to execute)*

- Four independent lifecycles — `idea_status`, `production_status`,
  `approvals.state`, `creative_metrics.verdict` — with four color systems and
  no single "where is this concept right now" view. A concept can be
  `Testing` + `Delivered` + `Approved` + `KILL` simultaneously.
- Three synonyms for CPT ≤ target: "Hit" (`src/components/IdeasList.tsx:36`),
  "On target ✓" (`src/components/client/ContentCard.tsx:74`), "Graduated"
  (`/performance`). Standardize on the client's own vocabulary (Graduated).
- The Ideas footer computes Hit/Miss from `creative_performance`
  (`src/app/(staff)/ideas/page.tsx:41-49`) while `/performance` renders
  `creative_metrics` verdicts — the same concept can read "Miss" on one page
  and "Graduated" on the other. Read one source (the verdict module,
  `src/lib/metrics/verdict.ts`, exists for exactly this).

## 5. Navigation gaps *(ready to execute)*

- **Back links are role-based, not origin-based**: the concept brief always
  returns staff to `/ideas` (`src/app/creatives/[id]/page.tsx:31-35`), even
  when opened from This Week, Review, or Winners. Pass an origin param or use
  history-aware back.
- **Nav highlight clears entirely on `/creatives/[id]`**
  (`src/components/NavLink.tsx:6-8`).
- **Staff "Queue" nav item is empty by design** for admins/editors (only shows
  your own assignments, `src/app/queue/page.tsx:34`) — either hide it for
  non-creators or make it an all-assignments view for staff.
- **No sign-out control** anywhere (`src/components/AppNav.tsx:45-47`).
- **Staff previewing the client portal keep the staff nav** with no
  exit-preview affordance; client sub-pages are only reachable via the home
  page cards (`src/app/client/page.tsx:68-72`).
- **Ideate → "Add to This Week" with no open cycle** drops the concept into
  Ideas with a toast (`src/components/IdeateWorkspace.tsx` `addConcept`) but no
  link to create a cycle. Link the toast to `/this-week`.

## 6. Duplicated helpers that will drift *(ready to execute)*

- The `famName`/`one()` array-unwrap helper is re-implemented in ~7 server
  pages (`ideas`, `this-week`, `queue`, `review`, `winners`, `creatives/[id]`,
  `client/data`).
- `STATUS_PILL` idea-status color maps exist in three copies
  (`IdeasList.tsx:23-28`, `creatives/[id]/page.tsx:18-23`,
  `client/ideas/page.tsx:7-12`).

Extract both into `src/lib/` (display maps could live beside
`src/lib/metrics/verdict.ts`, which already models the pattern).
